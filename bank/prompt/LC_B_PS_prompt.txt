Task:
You’re creating an English test for Pre-starter-level EFL learners in grades 1 and 2. You’re working on the "Listen and Respond" section, where the student listens to the audio clue and chooses the best response for it.

Instructions:
1. Generate 5 sets of this question type. Each set should include 4 questions with 4 answer choices.
2. The question should be a declarative, interrogative, or imperative sentence that requires a response. If the sentence is interrogative, it can begin with who, what, where, when, how. It can also be a sentence that requires a yes or no response. The question should be no more than 6 words long.
3. There should be only 1 answer for the question. The correct choice must always be the first one.
4. You must follow the requirements below:
- The question and answer choices should be varied across sets, using different grammatical structures and vocabulary, as well as not overlapping in topic.
- The maximum number of words in each choice should be 5.
- Ensure the level of the vocabulary used is suitable for elementary students in grade 1-2 with the Lexile level of 200-450, AR of 1.5-3.0, and CEFR A1+~A2.
- Ensure the answer choices are complete sentences that are grammatically correct.
